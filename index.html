GAMEMODES

Servers and Games in which we host

SKYBLOCK

Our Skyblock server has a spawner and crop farming based economy, with the addition of warping to mines and animal farms, you can be sure to increase your bank balance using /shop! Events happen every hour in the arena, be careful when collecting your envoy chests, as they might come with a death sentence! Visit the Outpost of the Arena and compete for OP rewards that you can use to boost your island's value and economy. When you visit the mines, be aware of the zombies who will give it their all when protecting their ores, when you die, you do lose half of your money!

OP PRISON

Explore the mines, Level up you're pickaxe, Rank up and escape the prison. Create a Cell and store you're wealth with your teammates!

Survival Multiplayer (SMP)

We host a SMP Server too! Based on 1.16.4, With default terrain, Community Events, Optimised proformance, Skills, Custom Commands and More! Check it out on noodlesmp.ga
